Visimus: Music Visualization

This page has a few web apps for music visualization developed by the Genos team.

  • Matrix: a simple app to search for notes in a 12-tone matrix.
  • Contour: an app to calculate and plot musical contour operations. It's a web interface for MusiContour.
  • Harmony: a mockup of a future app to show voice leading possibilities visualization.
